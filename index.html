about

Innovative Computer Engineering Students' Society (i-CES) is established with the strong motive to educate, promote, explore ,research and compete in the field of Computer Engineering. It has been providing platform to students who show keen interest in Computer programming and related projects.It also conducts seminars, presentations, documentaries shows and Competitions timely with aim to enhance the engineering skill of technical students in more practical way.When its about National and Local level Programming competitions,Innovative Computer Engineer's Society (i-CES) is one of the best representative of Western Region Campus.

ICON

Icon is an annual journal published with the collection of final year project report memo of both final year BCT & BEX students of Paschimanchal Campus. The first Icon had around 150 copies printed which included final year projects and memo of BCT/074 batch . The Icon vol-6 had combined the final year project of both BCT/074 and BEX/074. It included a total of 23 projects in which 12 were from BCT and 11 were from BEX. Icon is all about giving the idea for the projects to the next batch students and enhancing the current final batch students to do better and prosper their thoughts on their projects.

  • No. Of Prints: 150
  • Inaugurated On : 2080/02/23
  • Inaugurated By: Department Of Electronics and Computer Engineering

CodeCamp 2018

The mega event of the FSU technical festival “CodeCamp 2018” was successfully conducted through Ashad 2-4,2074 by i-CES in collaboration with COTS Nepal. It was national level event in which the participants were not only from Pokhara but also from the capital Kathmandu. It was a hackathon on the theme “Human Aid” in which the various eager teams submitted their ideas and only a handful of 12 ideas were selected. The participating 12 teams were required to present their demo ideas during the final day of event by coding on first two days under mentorship which was judged by very insightful people of our field. There were also various prizes under many theme won by many teams.

Build And Deploy NodeJS Apps with Microsoft Azure

Innovative Computer Engineering Students' Society (i-CES) brought three day workshop to build and deploy Node.js Apps with Microsoft Azure. You will learn to build a web application using Node.JS, a javascript framework. You will also learn how to deploy your web app to Microsoft Azure for your friends to see!
GO TO https://www.facebook.com/events/291886618406604/ to view glimpses of the event.

In this workshop, participants will explore the basics of Node.js and Express by making a HackerLog web app. You’ll guide them through setting up their JavaScript development environments, installing Visual Studio Code, downloading and testing an app locally, and seamlessly deploying it with Microsoft Azure.
